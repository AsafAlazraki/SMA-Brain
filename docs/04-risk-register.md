# Tony's Brain — Risk Register

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Voice doesn't feel seamless** (latency, false barge-ins, jargon mangling) — the sponsor's #1 bar | Med | High | Research-backed stack (WebRTC transport, semantic turn detection, Krisp-class noise cancellation, sentence-streamed TTS); latency budget engineered per component; bake-off with real workshop audio before committing vendors; S8 acceptance gate includes measured p50 < 1.5s | Asaf |
| 2 | **iOS Safari voice traps** (AEC self-hearing, PWA mic issues, backgrounding kills session) | High | Med | Follow documented mitigations: never bare-AudioContext playback while capturing; gesture-gated start; resume handling; treat installed-PWA voice as degraded path (deep-link to Safari tab if needed); explicit device test matrix in S8 | Asaf |
| 3 | **Catalog scrape brittleness** (Joomla/VirtueMart, dated markup, robots timeouts) | Med | Med | Idempotent resumable scraper + manual CSV import fallback; ask Tony for backend export access (preferred); scheduled re-scrape with diff alerts rather than blind overwrite | Asaf |
| 4 | **Tony's approval bandwidth** — queue piles up, brain stales, he disengages | Med | High | Queue UX designed for 10-second decisions (card stack, one-tap, keyboard); batch approvals; weekly "5 minutes of approvals" nudge; blurt mode keeps capture effortless so the habit sticks | Tony/Asaf |
| 5 | **Staff adoption** — old habits (ring Tony, dig through emails) persist | Med | High | On-a-call co-pilot designed around their real moment of need; email drafter saves visible time day 1; launch with Tony's endorsement + 1-page guide; usage dashboard shows wins | Tony |
| 6 | **Wrong answers reaching customers** (hallucinated prices/policies via email) | Low-Med | High | Grounding contract (citations mandatory for prices/policies/specs), confidence flags on drafts, staff review before send, eval suite regression-tests golden answers, corrections loop closes gaps fast | Asaf |
| 7 | **Knowledge conflicts/staleness** (price changes, discontinued models) | Med | Med | Conflict detection at approval time; product data refreshed by scheduled scrape; cards carry provenance + dates; "stale check" report (cards > 12 months old, admin review list) | Tony |
| 8 | **Customer PII in the learning loop** | Med | Med | Mining extracts topics only; distiller instructed to strip names/addresses; PII scrub pass before queue display; email text retention policy (12 months default); internal-only visibility default for anything email-derived | Asaf |
| 9 | **API cost creep** (voice minutes + big-model tokens) | Med | Low-Med | Two-tier model routing; prompt caching; usage_events dashboard with monthly budget alert; TTS sentence caching for repeated phrases | Asaf |
| 10 | **Public-phase prompt injection / data leak** (Phase 2) | High (if unmitigated) | High | Visibility enforced in SQL (anon RPCs), not prompts; separate endpoint + persona; red-team gate before launch; rate limiting; no tools beyond retrieval for anon | Asaf |
| 11 | **Single-founder knowledge concentration** (Tony unavailable → project stalls) | Low | High | This is the project's raison d'être — front-load teach sessions on unique-to-Tony knowledge (SMA-brand mods, policies, selection opinions) in month 1 | Tony |
| 12 | **Netlify Functions limits** (SSE duration, cold starts) bite chat or voice | Med | Med | Chat SSE validated on Netlify streaming functions at S4; voice runs on the dedicated realtime path per 05 (LiveKit-class infra), not Netlify; fallback: small always-on service (Fly.io Sydney) — decision gate documented | Asaf |
| 13 | **Vendor lock-in / vendor churn** (voice AI market moves fast) | Med | Low-Med | Adapter interfaces for STT/TTS/embeddings; knowledge base exportable to Markdown; transport layer isolated behind lib/voice | Asaf |
| 14 | **Scope creep before v1 ships** (live-call listening, public bot, integrations) | High | Med | Delivery plan gates; new ideas land in a "later" list in the PRD; sponsor sign-off per milestone | Asaf |

Review cadence: risks 1–2 at S8, risk 10 before P2 launch, rest monthly.
