## 2. History & People

His work has been credited by companies around the world." ([About Us](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/about-us))

## 4. Services

- **On-site service "Australasia"-wide / Queensland-wide**, around customer schedules, for "business and home users," with **after-hours service** for commercial clients ([About Us](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/about-us); [On-Site Service](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/on-site-service); [Technical Help](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/technical-help)). "SMA services all brands of machines both onsite and offsite" ([Terms](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/terms-a-conditions-of-sale)).
- **Service contracts / preventative maintenance programs** (Sleepmaker testimonial credits a PM program with significantly reduced breakdowns) ([Technical Help](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/technical-help)).
- **Virtual Technician** (video-link): VT1 "Learn The Basics" $95+GST; VT2 "Virtual Quotes" $95+GST; also virtual repairs/maintenance coaching and machine "health checks"; Australia-wide pickup/drop-off via freight network ([Virtual Technician](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/virtual-technician)).
- **Technical phone support** (commercial clients, on request); **30-day trade accounts** for approved applicants ([Technical Help](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/technical-help)).
- **Repairs booking** via online form covering every Australian state, machine type "Domestic | Industrial", payment cash/VISA/Mastercard/cheque/account ([Book Service or Repair](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/book-service-or-repair)).
- **Warranties:** 12-month manufacturer defect on goods; 6-month on portable bag closers & fabric cutters; 3-month on second-hand machinery; 7-working-day warranty on serviced work; on-site warranty work within 30km of Brisbane CBD (travel charged beyond, in 15-min increments); 2-year machine+motor on SMA-brand (clutch motor) machines ([Terms](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/terms-a-conditions-of-sale); [SMA Brand](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/what-is-the-sma-brand)).
- **Finance/purchase options:** leasing, layby, interest-free option on new machines (18+, ≥$25k p.a. income, permanent resident, clear credit), 30% deposit/balance-before-dispatch terms; "Instant Asset Write-Off" guidance article ([Leasing Options](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/leasing-options); [Layby](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/layby); [New machine enquiry](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/new-industrial-sewing-machine); [Terms](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/terms-a-conditions-of-sale)).
- **Setup/installation & training:** SMA-brand machines pre-set for the customer's threads/fabric/speed; unpacking phone support; on-site installation on request; operator training (Cook Medical and Prickers testimonials mention staff training) ([SMA Brand](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/what-is-the-sma-brand); [Testimonials](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/customer-testimonials)).
- **Fulfilment:** ships from Brisbane within 2 business days pending stock; loan/equivalent machines supplied during repairs ([About Us](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/about-us); [Testimonials](https://www.sewingmachinesaustralia.com.au/customer-testimonials)).
